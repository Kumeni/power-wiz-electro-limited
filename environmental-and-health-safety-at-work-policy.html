ENVIRONMENTAL, HEALTH AND SAFETY AT WORK POLICY

Power wiz Electro LTD is committed to the Environment, Health and Safety by undertaking and conducting businesses that are safe to our employees, the public and the environment in which we operate and comply with the statutory legislation and protocol.

To this end, it is our commitment to achieve Zero Injuries and environmental incidents. We believe accident prevention and efficient production go hand in hand. Safety and quality in all our engineering projects is not a goal but a requirement! We consider no phase of operation or administration as of greater importance than accident prevention.

Power wiz Electro LTD instigates a proactive loss prevention program to avoid human distress and financial loss before any project is started. Through inspections, meetings and incentive programs based on peer review we inform and educate employees regarding correct safety procedure and motivate them to make proper use of this information.

We deliver safe, environmental and quality management through our integrated systems of project plans, risk assessment and quality control. Strong site management audited quality control/ testing and innovative thinking combine to enable our firm to deliver projects of the highest quality.
